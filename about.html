About Us

Crowns Estates is a UK-rooted real estate company focused on connecting international investors with high-quality property opportunities in Saudi Arabia and The GCC. We understand both markets. Our strength lies in bridging the gap between overseas investors seeking secure, high-potential investments, and trusted developers offering premium real estate in one of the fastest-growing property markets in the world.

Our vision
is to become a trusted gateway for international investment into Saudi Arabia’s real estate sector, supporting the Kingdom’s growth while delivering strong, reliable opportunities to our clients.

Our Approach
We believe that successful real estate partnerships are built on:

Trust

Transparency

Clear communication

Who We Work With

At Crowns Estates, we work with two key partners:

Investors

We support international investors who are looking to: Enter the Saudi property market with confidence. Access vetted and reliable developments. Receive clear guidance throughout the process. We aim to simplify cross-border investment and provide a smooth, transparent experience from enquiry to completion.

Developers & Property Owners

We collaborate with developers and property owners in Saudi Arabia and The GCC by: Connecting them with serious, qualified international investors. Promoting their projects to a targeted UK audience. Supporting communication and relationship-building with overseas clients. Our goal is to help developers expand their reach beyond the local market and attract high-quality international demand.

As Saudi Arabia continues to open its doors to international investment, Crowns Estates is positioned to play a key role in connecting global investors with the Kingdom’s most exciting real estate opportunities.
